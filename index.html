About the Project

American Failure is a short animation depicting the enormous loss of life in the COVID-19 pandemic. With news about coronavirus focusing on the quarantine and the fierce disagreement on mask mandates, this project seeks to refocus our attention on the one irreversible consequence of our failure - the lives of fellow Americans. The American people deserve better than lies in this time of great loss. The American people deserve better than Donald Trump.

Viewing Issues

The American Failure animation is best viewed on a modern computer and web browser. If using a mobile device, view the animation in landscape orientation. If the frame rate is choppy or there are other technical problems, we recommend viewing the animation on our YouTube channel or connecting to a faster wifi network. Please feel free to reach out to me about any technical issues you experience - I will do my best to resolve it immediately.
